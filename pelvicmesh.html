Transvaginal mesh is a net-like implant that treats pelvic organ prolapse and urinary incontinence in women. But not all mesh implants are safe or reliable.

Before you decide on surgery, or if you are experiencing side effects from a pelvic mesh implant, get the facts from your local healthcare providers at our free dinner event.

Get Insight Into Your Unique Situation

At our dinner event, you’ll have exclusive access to the area’s experts in pelvic health outside of regular office hours. You’ll have an opportunity to ask questions and get insight into your unique situation.

As a result of this presentation, you can:

  • Banish confusion and stress. With guidance from our experts, you can make an informed decision about whether pelvic mesh surgery is right for you, and have the peace-of-mind in knowing that your decision is based on facts.
  • Get relief following complications from surgery. If you have complications from your pelvic mesh surgery, you’ll learn about your pain relief options and if you have cause to pursue legal action.
  • Save time. Searching for information on the internet can be time-consuming and inaccurate. On August 5, you can go straight to the experts who have the answers that you need.
  • Connect with your potential healthcare providers. If surgery is in your future but you do not yet have a physician, get to know our providers to find out if they would be the right fit for you.
